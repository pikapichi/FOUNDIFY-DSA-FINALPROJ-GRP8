Our Mission

Our mission is to create a seamless, efficient platform that helps campus community members quickly recover their lost items. By leveraging advanced data structures like HashMaps, Tries, and Binary Search Trees, we've built a smart matching system that connects lost items with their rightful owners.

We believe that technology should make life easier, and losing something valuable shouldn't be a source of ongoing stress. Our platform aims to turn the frustration of losing an item into the joy of finding it again.

Through intelligent matching algorithms and an intuitive user interface, we're committed to making the campus a more connected and helpful community where everyone looks out for each other.

What Makes Us Unique

Smart Matching Algorithm: Our similarity scoring system analyzes multiple factors including item name, description, category, location, and date to provide the most relevant matches.

Fast Search: Using Trie data structures, we provide instant autocomplete suggestions as you type, making it faster to report and search for items.

Organized Data: Binary Search Trees help us efficiently organize items by category and date, ensuring quick retrieval and filtering.

User-Friendly Design: Our modern, gradient-themed interface is both beautiful and functional, designed with Figma and implemented using the latest web technologies.

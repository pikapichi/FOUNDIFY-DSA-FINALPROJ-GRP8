Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username, full name, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Item Information: Descriptions, photos, categories, locations, and dates of lost/found items
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, search queries, and match views
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and operating system for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our lost-and-found matching services
  • To match lost items with found items using our similarity algorithms
  • To communicate with you about your reported items and potential matches
  • To ensure platform security, prevent fraud, and maintain service quality
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize system performance

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing algorithm (irreversible)
  • Secure database storage with SQLAlchemy ORM and SQLite
  • Regular security audits and system updates
  • Secure HTTPS connections for data transmission
  • Access controls and user authentication mechanisms

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Item information (descriptions, photos, locations) is visible to other registered users to facilitate item recovery. Reporter names are displayed to enable communication between finders and owners.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data and item history at any time through your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Update, correct, or modify your information as needed
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Request data export in portable format

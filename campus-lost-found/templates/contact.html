Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a lost item?

Click "Report Lost Item" from the navigation menu, fill in the details including description, location, date, and optional photo. Our system will automatically search for matches using our intelligent algorithm.

What if I find an item on campus?

Report it as a "Found Item" with as much detail as possible including photos. The owner will be notified automatically if there's a potential match based on our similarity scoring algorithm.

How does the matching work?

Our advanced algorithm compares items based on five factors: name similarity (40%), description (20%), category match (20%), location match (15%), and date proximity (5%). You'll see match scores from 0-100% with a minimum 30% threshold.
